Main Content

  1. Introduction

    FPT University is a private university in Vietnam. It is a member of FPT Corporation and has been the first university in Vietnam to apply the CDIO (Conceive-Design-Implement-Operate) model to its engineering education. The university is located in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang, Can Tho, and Hai Phong.

  2. History

    FPT University was established in 2006 under the management of FPT Education, a member of FPT Corporation. The university was founded with the mission of providing high-quality human resources to the IT industry in Vietnam. In 2010, FPT University became the first university in Vietnam to apply the CDIO model to its engineering education. The CDIO model is an innovative educational framework that focuses on developing students' engineering skills through project-based learning and real-world experiences. Since then, FPT University has been recognized as a pioneer in engineering education in Vietnam and has received numerous awards for its innovative approach to teaching and learning.
